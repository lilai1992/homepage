Sunrise at Mount Wutai 2017-12-23


This photo was taken at Mount Wutai (五台山) in Shanxi Province, China. At that time, Ning Guo with me watched the sunrise.

Mount Wutai is a sacred Buddhist site. It is the home of the Bodhisattva of wisdom, Mañjuśrī or “文殊” (Wénshū).
