About me

I am currently a research assistant at School of Mathematical Sciences in Fudan University (Shanghai, China). I obtained a master degree in mathematics from Tsinghua University in June 2020, under supervision of Pin Yu.

I am interested in transcendental number theory and wave equations. Recently I worked on irrationality of odd zeta values.
